Jeff's Story

Jeff's Story

Growing up Jeff always loved stories. Whether it was books, movies, or TV, he saw that stories could open up your imagination and show you the world from someone else’s eyes. That is why he chose to attend USC for their unique Business of Cinematic Arts program: he believed stories could change the world.

However, working for three years in the entertainment industry, Jeff saw unique films he was passionate about beat out by formulaic superhero sequels. Realizing financiers, not creatives, control ideas he joined Bloomberg to learn about investing so he could one day use his knowledge to help groundbreaking projects find financing.

Jeff views crowdfunding as a way to do just that. He is pursuing a role in Product Management with the end goal of one day developing a platform that will change how capital flows through our economy. Jeff’s experiences at Bloomberg have furnished him with a deep knowledge of finance, passion for technology, and a confidence in his ability to lead change.
